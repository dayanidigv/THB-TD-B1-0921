What is a Promise?

A Promise is like making a deal with someone:

  • Pending: "I promise to do this task" (working on it)
  • Fulfilled: "I kept my promise!" (task completed successfully)
  • Rejected: "Sorry, I couldn't keep my promise" (task failed)

Real Life Example:

When you order pizza:

  • Pending: Pizza shop is making your pizza
  • Fulfilled: Pizza delivered to your door
  • Rejected: Shop ran out of ingredients
